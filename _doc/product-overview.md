# Product Overview: EventFlow

## Purpose
A comprehensive event management platform for professional planners to orchestrate venues, vendors, guests, and schedules in one unified workspace.

## Positioning
The "mission control" for event planners who need to eliminate chaos and deliver flawless high-stakes events.

## Target Audience
- Professional Event Planners
- Wedding Coordinators
- Corporate Event Managers

## Core Strategic Principles
- **Clarity over Chaos**: Unified view of all moving parts.
- **Precision Timelines**: Minute-by-minute scheduling.
- **Vendor & Venue Hub**: Centralized contact and contract management.
- **Guest Experience**: Seamless list management and RSVPs.

## Scope (MVP)
- **Venue Management**: Track locations, capacities, and layout details.
- **Vendor Management**: Directory for caterers, decorators, AV, etc.
- **Guest Lists**: Manage invites, dietary requirements, and seating.
- **Timelines**: Real-time event day schedules.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Overview of multiple active events.

## Brand & Tone
- Professional, reliable, elegant, and calm.
- Colors: Deep Navy, Gold Accents, Clean White.
